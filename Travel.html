Book Your Room

Staying at Moon Palace

  • Food and drinks are included throughout your stay, with access to 17 restaurants and 13 bars.
  • All wedding events will include dining and a private bar.
  • No deposit required — pay closer to the trip.
  • Each room receives $250 resort credit.
  • The hotel is approximately 20 minutes from Cancun Airport.

Important Notes

  • Please remember to arrange your free airport transfers when booking.
  • Guests staying elsewhere will incur a $200 per person, per event fee.
  • Room location requests are welcomed, but cannot be guaranteed. Priority will be given to older guests and families.
