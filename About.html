Order of Events

Here’s an overview of the celebrations across the four days. We are carefully planning the timings so you can enjoy as much of the resort as possible, with full details of locations and schedules shared when you arrive.

We want everyone to relax and truly enjoy the celebrations with us, so we only ask for a few small considerations:

  • The resort is quite large, so please allow time to arrive promptly for each event.
  • During the ceremonies, we kindly ask that guests remain seated and observe silence and respect.
  • We ask that children are supervised and not running around during these moments.

Welcome Party

Sunday 14th February

Spend Valentine’s Day with us as we begin the celebrations with sunset drinks, mehndi artists, and plenty of games and laughter.

Vidhi & Joint Haldi

Monday 15th February

We’ll start the day with the Vidhi, a traditional blessing ceremony. As it’s an early one, feel free to join if you wish — we’ll then gather for the Haldi ceremony and celebrations together.

Jago

Monday 15th February

Join us for our joint Jago ceremony filled with dancing, music, and all the wedding vibes.

Hindu Wedding Ceremony

Tuesday 16th February

Join us as we marry in a traditional Gujarati wedding ceremony.

Civil Ceremony

Wednesday 17th February

A short and sweet civil ceremony.

Reception

Wednesday 17th February

A joyful evening of dinner, speeches, and celebrating together.
